Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist-

->Do’s:
.Do think before you post — ensure your message is respectful and appropriate.
.Do protect your privacy — use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
.Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
.Do maintain a positive digital presence — share content that reflects well on you.
.Do respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy, constructive discussions.

->Don’ts:
.Don’t share personal details publicly such as address, phone number, or financial information.
.Don’t post when angry or emotional, as it may lead to regrettable content.
.Don’t engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or negative comments.
.Don’t overshare college or workplace information that may be confidential.
.Don’t upload inappropriate photos or videos that could harm your reputation later.
